Enterprise Project Management Office (EPMO) Accessibility Requirements

This is a preliminary checklist that needs to be completed to ascertain if your project has accessibility requirements. The checklist purpose is to help identify potential accessibility requirements and acts as a point of first contact for project manager with the IT Accessibility Office (ITAO).

Instructions

Please follow each step in order.

  1. Review Supporting Information section related to accessibility standards and policies.
  2. Complete the Accessibility Checklist .
  3. Print document to PDF and save as EPMO_[project name]_[date].
  4. Upload your EPMO_[project name]_[date] document in our EPMO request folder.
  5. Submit a request in our Request Management Tool.

Accessibility Checklist

Please complete the accessibility checklist to the best of your knowledge by selecting all checkboxes that apply to your ICT solution to develop your project. Please note while completing the checklist, you may need advice from the IT team responsible developing your project.
