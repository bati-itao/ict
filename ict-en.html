Enterprise Project Management Office (EPMO) Accessibility Requirements

This is a preliminary checklist that needs to be completed to ascertain if your project has accessibility requirements. The checklist purpose is to help identify potential accessibility requirements and acts as a point of first contact for project manager with the IT Accessibility Office (ITAO).

Instructions

Please follow each step in order.

  1. Review Supporting Information section related to accessibility standards and policies.
  2. Complete Accessibility Checklist .
  3. Print save document as PDF EPMO_[project name]_[date].
  4. Upload your EPMO_[project name]_[date] document in our EPMO request folder, please submit a request in our Request Management Tool. If your require assistance, please send an email to the ITAO generic email address .

Accessibility Checklist

Please complete the accessibility checklist to the best of your knowledge by selecting all checkboxes that apply to your ICT solution developing your project. Please note while completing the checklist, you may need advice from the IT team responsible developing your project.
